BUSINESS ROADMAP

Barangay eBlotter System

1. RESEARCH & REQUIREMENTS ASSESSMENT

EARLY 2025

  • Conduct detailed surveys and interviews with officials and residents.
  • Analyze current manual processes and legal compliance needs (Data Privacy, Katarungang Pambarangay).
  • Define the initial minimum viable product (MVP) scope.

2. SOLUTION CONCEPTUALIZATION & DESIGN

MID 2025

  • Define key features (e.g., instant reporting, role-based dashboards, AI analytics engine).
  • Design System Architecture and UI/UX for mobile-first compatibility.
  • Establish data security and encryption protocols.

3. DEVELOPMENT & PROTOTYPE TESTING

LATE 2025

  • Build the initial version of the system (MVP).
  • Conduct rigorous Alpha and Beta testing with staff and core residents.
  • Refine and debug the system based on usability and performance metrics.

4. OPTIMIZATION & STAFF TRAINING

EARLY 2026

  • Refine system features and optimize database performance.
  • Conduct comprehensive training sessions for all Barangay staff and Lupong Tagapamayapa.
  • Formalize system adoption protocols with the Barangay Council.

5. LAUNCH & EXPANSION

MID 2026

  • Publicly launch the Barangay eBlotter System and commence official operation.
  • Begin monitoring long-term system performance and adoption rate.
  • Plan for Phase 2 integration with external Local Government Unit (LGU) databases.
